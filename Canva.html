1. WRITTING EBOOKS

Ever wondered how you could monetize your talents? Can you make money with Canva? Absolutely! Many authors consistently earn over $1000 monthly by writing and publishing ebooks on platforms like Amazon, Draft2Digital, Selar, and others. You don't have to be a Canva expert to start earning with it. To make money with Canva, simply head to the App Store or Google Play Store to download the Canva app, or visit www.canva.com to register with your email. After logging in, type "ebook" in the search bar of your Canva dashboard to find ebook cover templates. Customize the cover design and start writing your ebook. Add pages as needed until your ebook is complete, then download it as a PDF and publish to start earning in dollars.

2. PRINT ON DEMAND

An avenue for generating income with Canva is through designing products for print-on-demand. Utilizing Canva, you have the capability to create or revamp products like T-shirts, shorts, cups, chairs, phone covers, headsets, and more to your liking. Once designed, you can list these items on platforms like Etsy and connect your Etsy account to Printify. This allows your customers to print your products on demand, providing a seamless transaction process.

6. CREATION OF ANIMATION VIDEOS

Creating animation videos has never been simpler than with Canva. By accessing Canva's platform, you can easily initiate the process. Begin by selecting the "mobile video" option, followed by choosing a blank background to serve as your canvas. Navigate to the "apps" section located on the left menu bar, where you'll find various options including uploads, text, elements, and more. Within this section, search for "NeiroAi" to select a suitable AI Avatar to represent yourself in the animation. While Canva offers remarkable tools for animation creation, you may need to procure voiceovers from alternate sources and potentially incorporate background music to enhance the appeal of your animated video.
