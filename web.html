20 HIGH PAYING WEBSITES FOR STUDENTS IN 2024/2025.

1. TEACHABLE

Teachable is a platform where users can create and sell online courses. Students can create courses on topics they are knowledgeable about and earn money when people enroll in their courses.

2. YOUTUBE

YouTube allows users to earn money through its Partner Program. Students can create and upload videos to YouTube, enable monetization, and earn money from ads displayed on their videos.

3. FIVERR

Fiverr is another freelance platform where students can offer services starting at $5. They can create "gigs" for various tasks and receive payments for completed orders.

4. UPWORK

Upwork is a freelance platform where students can offer their skills in areas like writing, graphic design, programming, and more. They can create a profile, bid on projects, and get paid for completed work.

5. RESPONDENT

Respondent is a platform that connects users with paid research studies and focus groups. Students can participate in studies on various topics and earn money for their time and feedback.

6. GIGWALK

Gigwalk is a platform where users can find local "gigs" or short-term jobs in their area. Students can complete gigs such as mystery shopping, product testing, and more, and earn money for completed tasks.

7. CLICKWORKER

Clickworker is a platform where users can complete microtasks, such as data entry, web research, and more, for payment. Students can choose tasks that fit their skills and earn money for completing them.

8. SLICE THE PIE

Slice the Pie pays users for reviewing music, fashion, and other products. Students can listen to music, write reviews, and earn money for their feedback.

9. CHEGG TUTORS

Chegg Tutors is a platform where students can offer tutoring services in various subjects. They can create a profile, set their own rates, and earn money for tutoring sessions.

10. REV

Rev is a platform where users can earn money by transcribing audio, captioning videos, and translating documents. Students can work on tasks that fit their skills and earn money for completed work.

11. USERTESTING

UserTesting pays users for testing websites and providing feedback. Students can sign up, complete website tests, and earn money for their feedback.

12. TASKRABBIT

TaskRabbit is a platform that connects users with local freelance labor. Students can offer services such as errand running, furniture assembly, and more, and get paid for completed tasks.

13. AMAZON MECHANICAL TURK

Amazon Mechanical Turk (MTurk) is a platform where users can complete small tasks, known as Human Intelligence Tasks (HITs), for payment. Students can choose tasks that fit their skills and earn money for completing them.

14. SWAGBUCKS

Swagbucks is a rewards program that pays users for completing online surveys, watching videos, shopping online, and more. Students can earn points (Swagbucks) and redeem them for gift cards or cash via PayPal.

15. SURVEY JUNKIE

Survey Junkie pays users for taking online surveys. Students can sign up, complete surveys on various topics, and earn points that can be redeemed for cash or gift cards.
